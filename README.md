# MoviesFinder
MoviesFinder is a cross-platform mobile app (Android and iOS) that allows to consult all the information of the last films released in the cinema.

## About
When you think cinema or before going to the cinema, you ask yourself the following questions:
- What are the latest films released in the cinema?
- What was this movie that I had seen some time ago?
- What is the audience's rating of this movie I heard about?

The answer to all your questions is MoviesFinder. With this mobile app, you can search for movies from its title and also check out the latest best movies released at the cinema.
You like a movie? You can immediately put it as a favorite in your profile, and MoviesFinder will keep it in your memory for you.
Want to share movie titles with your friends? It's easy, MoviesFinder allows sharing Android / iOS with your friends.

You can also customize MoviesFinder by adding your profile picture.

With MoviesFinder, you'll know everything about cinema movies.
